Hello I’m Kadwama

I am a detail-oriented, performance driven and highly enthusiastic user experience & user interface designer that is passionate about evolving technology and how to use it to create digital user centered products to solve problems around me and the world at large. Strongly experienced in user research, journey mapping, building personas and user flows, infographics interface design, rapid prototyping and usability testing, summing up to build appealing human centered products that creates a seamless user experience.
New and complex technologies are been integrated into digital products to solve problems, my role as a user experience designer is to ensure that users are able to use these products to solve their problems without getting confused or feeling dumb at any time, and I achieve these by using a simple design process.
My years of hands-on experience and persistent growth in the user experience & user interface design field has helped me acquire skills like; speed, accuracy, creative thinking and problem solving ability, that enables me to create usable and appealing designs.
