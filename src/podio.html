PODIO APP : A new approach to podcast

Product Overview

Podio is a mobile appication that allows users to have an enjoyable wide vatrieties of features unlike the conventional podcast we are used to. Podio allows users to listen to podcast from different podcasters under different categories. Adding to that, users can like, comment and share podcast they really enjoyed.

The Problem

Podcast is a globally accepted means of sharing stories, information, experiences and content through digital audio files. Now most existing podcast applications and platforms only allows podcast users to record, listen and share podcast thereby limiting the users enjoyable experience..

The Solution

PODIO our new approach to podcast allows users to to listen, download, share, like, comment, save for later and bookmark podcast. Also from the broadcasting side of the mobile application, PODIO allows a user to either live record or select a recorded audio file from their files and share. Thereby giving users limitless options to explore audio shraring .
