Discovering the problem

Various loans applications are on the increase today and we have noticed a thread of over charging of profit percentage on loans, and also lack of proper tool to track, plan and budget spending expenditures

About Ilator

Ilator enables users to calculate loan interest rate, track spending expenditure, set spending limit and see all transaction history. This help in avoiding over payment of loan interest, and proper management of spending and expenditures.
