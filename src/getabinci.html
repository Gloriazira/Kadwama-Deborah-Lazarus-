Process

Discovering the problem

There are various restaurants and eatries here in Yola, Nigeria but no existing food delivering platform that enables users to order food from the comfort of thier homes or offices. It has been existing problem that was not so obvious to see or point out. A couple of tech enthusiast were hungry in the office and so much task to finish up that they could not spare time to step out and buy food themselves, and that was how the GetAbinci idea was concieved. It’s not entirely new but it’s new here in Yola, Northeast Nigeria.

About GetAbinci

GetAbinci is a mobile applicatio that connects users to their favourite eatries and restaurants and enables users to order from any of the eatries and restuarents registered with GetAbinci and get it delivered to them. GetAbinci targets potential users like entrprneurs and workers who do not have enough time to stepput and buy food or cook themsleves
